Pokemon Platformer

Pika Storm Run

A self-contained 25-level platform game inspired by Pokemon and classic side-scrollers. Pick Pikachu, Charmander, or Squirtle, catch storm sparks, spend skill charges, and avoid shadow Pokemon.

Controls

A D or Left Right to move

W Up or Space to jump

X to use your Pokemon skill

Enter to use the main action button

Choose Your Pokemon
